My Hobby

My hobby is to take apart stuff, figure out how it works, and then put it back together(sometimes). Figure out how it works, take it apart, put it together, make some tweaks. All the works. I even found my dream career already! My dream career is to become a software engineer. A software engineer makes the code, makes the circuit boards, and all the other fancy stuff.
